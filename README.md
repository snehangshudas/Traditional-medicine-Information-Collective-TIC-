# Traditional medicine Information Collective (TIC)
TIC (Traditional medicine Information Collective) is an integrated framework of traditional medicine guidelines suggested by government of different countries. All the herbal formulations mentioned here are strictly issued by the governments and no other guidelines are issued here. These guidelines by different countries have been given a green flag by World Health Organisation (WHO) to test their efficiencies at different stages of disease development to combat COVID-19. 
Anyone can contribute to this database with valid herbal formulations approved by their governments.
